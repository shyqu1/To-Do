FAQ

Is the app free to use?

Yes, our services are completely free. You can use any function without paying money.

Is a account required to use your services?

No, you don't need an account to manage your to do's. If you want to see your notes on multiple devices at once, creating a account is required.

How do I reset my password?

Click “Forgot password” from the login page or “Change password” from your profile page. A reset link will be emailed to you.

How can I contact you?

You can send us a message via our contact formular or just send a email (which you can find in our about-page) with your request.

How can I choose between different themes?

You can change the theme anytime in the settings.

Is the app available in several languages?

Our app is currently available in English, German and Russian. We're working on more available languages in the future.

Where can I download the app?

You can download the app in the Google Play Store or use our web-app. If we release the app in the AppStore (iOS) is not clear yet.
